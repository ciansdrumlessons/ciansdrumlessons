Biography

From a very young age, KeyN showed an aptitude for playing music on a range of instruments, eventually deciding to pursue drums professionally. Beginning in 2004 and whilst still in school, he embarked on playing in various bands and musical theatre productions throughout Cork.

In 2016, KeyN was chosen to fill the much sought-after position as a member of the house band for a large-scale Irish music and dance production in Disney World, Orlando, Florida. Upon returning to Cork in 2017, he first completed his undergraduate degree from the Cork School of Music and subsequently earned a first-class honours MA in music performance, also from the Cork School of Music, in 2019.
