Biography

From a very young age, Cian showed an aptitude for playing music on a range of instruments, eventually deciding to pursue drums professionally. Beginning in 2004 and whilst still in school, he embarked on playing in various bands and musical theatre productions throughout Cork. This led to Cian’s extensive experience in playing for numerous shows and events at a high level nationwide.

In 2016, Cian was chosen to fill the much sought-after position as a member of the house band for a large-scale Irish music and dance production in Disney World, Orlando, Florida. Upon returning to Cork in 2017, he first completed his undergraduate degree from the Cork School of Music and, in quick succession, subsequently earned a first-class honours MA in music performance, also from the Cork School of Music, in 2019. This involved in-depth study of jazz music, advanced techniques, and ensemble performance.

In addition to his third-level studies, Cian has received masterclasses from Dave Weckl, Ash Soan, and Rod Quinn.

Cian’s accolades have awarded him an excellent reputation as a drummer and, as such, he is in high demand for his playing. Included amongst his recent performances are a tour with Finbar Wright of the Irish Tenors and a jazz quintet performance supporting Vieux Farke Toure in Cyprus Avenue at The Cork Jazz Festival.

Cian passionately enjoys playing with local Rock and Roll bands and attending various jazz jam sessions both locally and in Europe. Cian has been teaching since 2017, both privately and in various music schools.
