Biography

In 2016, Cian was chosen to fill the much sought-after position as a member of the house band for a large-scale Irish music and dance production in Disney World, Orlando, Florida. Upon returning to Cork in 2017, he first completed his undergraduate degree from the Cork School of Music and, in quick succession, subsequently earned a first-class honours MA in music performance, also from the Cork School of Music, in 2019. This involved in-depth study of jazz music, advanced drum technique and the organisation and performance of several public recitals.

In addition to his third-level studies, Cian has received masterclasses from Dave Weckl (Chick Corea, Simon/Garfunkel), Ash Soan (The Voice UK, Adele, Seal) and Rod Quinn (Van Morrison, Gilbert O’Sullivan).

Cian’s accolades have awarded him an excellent reputation as a drummer and, as such, he is in high demand for his playing. Included amongst his recent performances are a tour with Finbar Wright of the Irish Tenors and a jazz quintet performance supporting Vieux Farke Toure in Cyprus Avenue at The Cork Jazz Festival.

Cian passionately enjoys playing with local Rock and Roll bands and attending various jazz jam sessions both locally and in Europe. He has been teaching since 2017, both privately and in various music schools including The Cork School Of Music. He is passionate and intuitive about the communicative needs of each individual student and greatly enjoys sharing the wisdom he has gathered, as he continues on his own journey of musical education and fulfilment.
